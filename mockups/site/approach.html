The four phases

A predictable arc from first conversation to lasting result. Timelines flex with scope, but the shape stays the same.

Discover & AssessWeeks 1 to 2

Interviews, current state assessment, process mapping, and opportunity identification.

Design & PrioritizeWeeks 2 to 4

Future state, roadmap, KPI framework, and a quick wins plan.

Implement & EnableMonths 2 to 4

Improvements, dashboards, automation, training, and change support.

Sustain & OptimizeOngoing

Performance reviews, KPI monitoring, and continuous refinement.

Phase 01

Discover & Assess

We start by understanding how the business actually runs today, not how the org chart says it should.

Listen first

Structured interviews with owners, leadership, and the people doing the day-to-day work, where the real friction shows up.

Map the current state

Process mapping across finance and operations to see how work, data, and decisions move through the business.

Find the opportunities

A prioritized view of where time, money, and clarity are leaking, ranked by effort and payback.

Phase 02

Design & Prioritize

A future state you can picture, with a roadmap that sequences the work so early wins fund the rest.

Define the future state

A practical target operating model for finance and operations: who does what, supported by which systems.

Build the roadmap

A phased plan that sequences improvements by impact, dependency, and payback, not by what is easiest.

Set the KPI framework

The handful of measures that will tell everyone whether the changes are working.

Agree the quick wins

Two or three changes we can deliver fast, to build momentum and prove the value early.

Phase 03

Implement & Enable

This is where IntiEdge is different. We stay in the work until it is live and your team can run it.

Deliver the improvements

Process redesign, controls, and reporting put into place, working in your real environment.

Stand up dashboards & automation

Live dashboards and automated workflows that replace manual assembly with reliable, repeatable output.

Train and support the change

Hands-on enablement so the people who own the work afterwards are confident, not just informed.

Phase 04

Sustain & Optimize

Improvements that hold. We keep an eye on the numbers and refine as the business evolves.

Review performance

Regular check-ins against the KPI framework to confirm the gains are real and durable.

Monitor the KPIs

Ongoing visibility so a slip is caught early, while it is still cheap to fix.

Refine continuously

Steady, incremental improvement as priorities shift, with engagement that scales up or down as you need.
