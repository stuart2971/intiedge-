Case Study 01

Finance Transformation Through AP, AR & Contract Automation

Growing multi-entity SaaS organization

Challenge

The organization relied on highly manual accounts payable, accounts receivable, and contract management processes.

  • AP invoices required manual routing and approvals.
  • Customer invoicing took up to three weeks after month-end.
  • Contracts were stored across multiple systems.
  • Limited visibility into recurring revenue and renewals.
  • Processes were becoming difficult to scale.

Solution

Led the end-to-end transformation by:

  • Mapping existing processes and identifying bottlenecks.
  • Redesigning workflows and standardizing approvals.
  • Implementing integrated AP and AR automation.
  • Centralizing contract management.
  • Working with Finance, Operations, IT, and software vendors.

Results

  • Reduced AP processing time by approximately 50%.
  • Reduced the invoicing cycle from three weeks to one.
  • Improved cash flow through faster billing.
  • Increased visibility into ARR and renewals.
  • Improved revenue recognition.
  • Built a scalable operating model.
